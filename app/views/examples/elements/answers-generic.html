What happens next?

New left hand drive vehicle from outside EU

We will send you a V55/4 and detailed instructions to complete it.

You will need these items:

  • ID (photocopies are acceptable)
  • Fee for vehicle tax and First registration (if applicable)
  • Certificate or Declaration of Newness
  • Type Approval (if applicable):
    • If you have an ECWVTA Certificate of Conformity which states left hand drive and metric units, they will need to be referred to VCA to get a mutual recognition certificate to show the correct hand of traffic and speedometer units.
    • If they cannot get an ECWVTA CoC from the manufacturer, they should be able to get an IVA certificate from DVSA)

You must also contact HMRC (using the NOVA service) and pay any taxes.
