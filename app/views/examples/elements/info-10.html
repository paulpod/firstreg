10. Commercial importers of new vehicles

Commercial importers can use a simpler process to license and register new vehicles with the Driver and Vehicle Licensing Agency (DVLA).

How it works

You can only register vehicles with type approval under the scheme.

Use the form V55/2, not form V55/4.

You don’t need to provide supporting evidence or complete the HM Revenue and Customs (HMRC) forms.

Apply to use the scheme

You can use the scheme if you:

  • are registered for VAT
  • can securely store certificates of conformity so they can be audited on request
If accepted, you’ll be responsible for:
  • proper use and secure storage of the V55/2 forms
  • sending the completed V55/2 forms to DVLA, on behalf of your customers

      • How to apply

      Email the Vehicle Policy Group at DVLA to get an application pack.
